💎 Gem & Mineral Guide

Collect geological treasures across the universe to fund your ship's evolution.

🛠️ Industrial Grade

(Iron, Copper, Coal, Titanium, Silicon)
Common materials found in asteroid belts. Reliable but low value. Good for early-game grinding.

🥇 Precious Metals

(Silver, Gold, Platinum, Palladium)
High-value metals that fetch a good price at docking stations. Often found near gas giants.

🔮 Crystal Clusters

(Quartz, Diamond, Emerald, Ruby, Sapphire)
Rare crystalline structures found in deep space. Extreme value and aesthetic brilliance.

☢️ Nuclear Elements

(Uranium, Plutonium, Helium-3)
Dangerous but highly sought after for reactor fuel. High risk, high reward.

✨ Exotic Matter

(Dark Matter, Antimatter, Neodymium)
Anomalous substances found near black holes and supernovae. The most valuable materials in the galaxy.

💰 How to use?

  • Fly over a glowing gem to collect it.
  • Gems automagically sell for **Credits** when they enter your hold.
  • Open the **Interstellar Dock (🛠️ UPGRADES)** to spend credits.
  • Upgrade your **Engines**, **Armor**, **Shields**, and **Radar** to survive deeper space!
